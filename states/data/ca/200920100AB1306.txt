											  Veterans: buildings, memorials, and cemeteries.Existing law authorizes the formation of memorial districts and commissions for the purposes of providing and maintaining veterans buildings, memorials, and cemeteries.This bill would state the intent of the Legislature to consolidate the duties and powers of the commissions formed for the purposes of providing and maintaining veterans buildings, memorials, and cemeteries under the auspices of advisory committees appointed by the California Veterans Board.Digest Key
