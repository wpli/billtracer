											  Veterans.Existing law requires the state to pay a death benefit of $10,000 to the surviving spouse or designated beneficiary of any member of the California National Guard, the State Military Reserve, or the Naval Militia who dies or is killed after March 1, 20003, in the performance of duty, as determined by the Military Department.This bill would make technical, nonsubstantive changes to this death benefit provision.Digest Key
