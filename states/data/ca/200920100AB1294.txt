						MonningFebruary 27, 2009 An act to amend Section 12935 of the Water Code, relating to water resources. LEGISLATIVE COUNSEL'S DIGESTAB 1294, as introduced, Monning.
											  State Water Resources Development System.The Department of Water Resources operates the State Water Resources Development System, commonly referred to as the State Water Project, in accordance with the California Water Resources Development Bond Act.This bill would make technical, nonsubstantive changes to a provision that authorizes the issuance of bond funds in the amount of $1,750,000,000 for the purposes of that bond act.
