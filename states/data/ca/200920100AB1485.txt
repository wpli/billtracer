						AndersonFebruary 27, 2009 An act relating to civil actions. LEGISLATIVE COUNSEL'S DIGESTAB 1485, as introduced, Anderson.
											  Civil actions: enforcement of judgments.Existing law allows for the enforcement of money judgments, judgments for the possession or sale of personal property or real property, and judgments requiring performance of an act, as provided.This bill would state the intent of the Legislature to amend the provisions relating to the enforcement of judgments.Digest Key
