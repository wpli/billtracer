						SmythFebruary 27, 2009 An act to amend Section 120190 of the Health and Safety Code, relating to public health. LEGISLATIVE COUNSEL'S DIGESTAB 1418, as introduced, Smyth.
											  Communicable diseases: reporting.Existing law requires a local health officer to immediately report by telegraph or telephone to the department every discovered or known case or suspect case of those diseases designated for immediate reporting by the department.This bill would also include electronic mail or electronic facsimile as permitted methods of reporting.Digest Key
