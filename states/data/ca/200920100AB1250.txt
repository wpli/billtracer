						VillinesFebruary 27, 2009 An act to amend Section 69504 of the Education Code, relating to student aid. LEGISLATIVE COUNSEL'S DIGESTAB 1250, as introduced, Villines.
											  Postsecondary education: state-funded student financial aid.Existing law establishes various student financial aid programs that are funded by the state or a public postsecondary educational institution. Existing law provides that provisions of law applicable to state funded need-based grants do not apply to regulate the awarding of scholarships that are privately funded.This bill would make a technical, nonsubstantive change to these provisions.Digest Key
