											  Forest practices: timber harvesting plans.The Z’berg-Nejedly Forest Practice Act of 1973 prohibits a person from conducting timber operations, as defined, unless a timber harvesting plan prepared by a registered professional forester has been submitted to the Department of Forestry and Fire Protection, and approved.This bill would make technical, nonsubstantive changes to those provisions.Digest Key
