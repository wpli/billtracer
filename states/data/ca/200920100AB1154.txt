						Audra StricklandFebruary 27, 2009 An act to add Section 33353.1 to the Education Code, relating to the California Interscholastic Federation. LEGISLATIVE COUNSEL'S DIGESTAB 1154, as amended, Audra Strickland.
											  California Interscholastic Federation: notice of sanctions.Existing law recognizes that the California Interscholastic Federation is a voluntary organization that consists of school and school-related personnel with responsibility for administering interscholastic athletic activities in secondary schools.This bill would require encourage the California Interscholastic Federation, and its sections, whenever it imposes they impose a sanction on an interscholastic team of a member school, to post
		online the name of the school, the team that has been sanctioned, the violation that has occasioned the sanction, and a description of the sanction itself.Digest Key
